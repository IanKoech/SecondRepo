Ingredients required to prepare Pilau Masala

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 3 table spoons of vegetable oil or olive oil(if health conscious)
  • 2 cups of chopped lamb
  • Half a teaspoon of pepper
  • Half a cup of finely chopped onions
  • 1 medium sized cinnamon stick
  • A table spoon of ginger paste
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • A table spoon of garlic paste
  • A teaspoon of turmeric
  • A quarter cup of coconut oil
  • A chicken flavored cube
  • A table spoon of coriander powder
  • 2 tablespoons of pilau Masala
  • Salt

    • Cooking procedure to prepare pilau masala

    1. Put a pan on the flame and heat oil in medium heatüî•
    2. Add the chopped lamb on the pan and stir until the color of the chickenlamb becomes golden-brownüçó
    3. Transfer the fried lamb to another vesselü•ò
    4. In the same pan, add oil again and all the spices, when the spices start crackling, add the chopped onions and green peppers
    5. Fry all the above ingredients until they turn golden brown üç≥
    6. Add the ginger and garlic paste. Stir until the garlic smell is gone. Add the turmeric and stir for a minute
    7. Add the fried lamb and rice into the pan and stir well
    8. In a separate bowl, add the coconut milk with the two cups of hot lamb broth
    9. Add the coconut mixture into the pan and stir wellü••
    10. Cover the pan and turn the heat into medium and wait for 20 minutes until the rice is dry
    11. Serve the pilau in a serving plate

    Pilau is golden brown in color and should have a barbeque smell and cinamon smell. Pilau has a tang smell.
